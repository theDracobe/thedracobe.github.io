Programable Macro Pad Workshop

Throughout this multi-part workshop, students learned various skills including PCB design, circuit theory, PCB manufacturing, microcontroller programming, keyboard matrix theory, advanced soldering, resin casting, silicone molding, 3D printing, and more. The series synthesized these skills into one extended project, a programable macro pad.

Students were highly engaged both inside and outside the workshops, returning to the spaces throughout the week to continue developing and customizing their macro pads using the 3D printers, CNC routers, and a variety of lower-resolution tools.
